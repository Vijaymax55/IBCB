About MCLF

Who We Are

MCLF is a community-owned institution formed by Self-Help Group (SHG) federations at the cluster level, operating under the National Rural Livelihoods Mission (NRLM), Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India.

  • Federated body of village-level SHG groups
  • Promotes financial inclusion and credit access
  • Facilitates skill development and livelihood support
  • Strengthens social accountability and governance
  • Supports women's leadership and empowerment
What We Do

Our Key Activities

We provide a range of support services to SHG members across the cluster to improve livelihoods and well-being.

💰

Financial Services

Facilitating bank linkages, credit access, and savings mobilisation for SHG members through formal financial institutions.

🌾

Livelihood Support

Promotion of agriculture, animal husbandry, and non-farm enterprises to diversify and strengthen household income.

📚

Capacity Building

Training and skill development programmes for SHG members, community resource persons, and federation leaders.

🏥

Social Development

Campaigns on health, nutrition, education, and sanitation to improve quality of life in rural communities.

📊

Monitoring & Governance

Ensuring transparent bookkeeping, audit, and democratic governance of SHG and federation operations.

🤝

Market Linkages

Connecting SHG producers to markets, supply chains, and government procurement schemes for fair pricing.
